Franchise Opportunities

Fibber Magee's is an institution. Famed for being the oldest Irish pub in the MENA region, we have established a reputation for being authentic, traditional and iconic. For over 30 years we have welcomed expats, tourists and business travellers into our pubs, with over 2 million guests supping more than 9 million pints of our award winning Guinness since our inception in 1996.

The Track Record

An Iconic Brand with decades of Success

  • 30 years and 9 million pints of Guinness
  • Robust financial returns for investors
  • Strategic resilience during economic challenges
  • Positive social impact in the UAE & UK through charitable contributions
The Opportunity

Opening Doors to Franchisees in 2026

Over the years, we have been approached by many institutional investors, publicans, businesses and families about taking on the Fibber's brand. And for years we have scratched our heads and toyed with this idea.

So from 2026, we have decided to open our doors to experienced, passionate and commercially minded franchisees.

Our Commitment

As the franchisor, we pledge the following

  • The shared use of our award winning, long established and popular brand, Fibber Magee's
  • A smooth, seamless and exciting onboarding process, working closely with our team while applying your own creative flair and ideas
  • Full legal and financial guidance on incorporation, licensing and permits
  • Access to the best suppliers and contractors in the market, with preferential group rates
  • Management support throughout your tenure
  • Most importantly, we help you build a business and venue you love, where the spirit of Fibber's drives superior, sustainable returns
